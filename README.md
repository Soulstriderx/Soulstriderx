### Projects

</div>

Here is a short (and very exhaustive) list of my past projects or collaborated projects with others that were uploaded
- [Financial Tracker](https://github.com/Soulstriderx/finance-tracker) `Kotlin / Firebase`
  - Mobile Semester 2 Project
  - This app allows you to track your finances, bills, budgets and comes with a pretty neat rollover system.

- [Manga Reader Archive](https://github.com/Soulstriderx/mangareader) `React / MongoDB`
  - Web Semester 2 Project
  - This allows you to upload and archive your mangas in a visual manner for local use. Instead of digging through a folder, you can now just browse your mangas with ease

- [Alumni App](https://github.com/Jeromee1/Alumni-Directory-Mobile-App) `Kotlin / Firebase`
  - Mobile Semester 2 Collaborative Project
  - I was in-charge of the back-end using Firebase
  - Alumni are able to register and await approval from admins as it enforces an internal membership system. Users are able to track each other through an internal profile and keep in touch.
